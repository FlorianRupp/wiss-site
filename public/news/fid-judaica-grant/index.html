We are very happy that our project proposal for an expert information servide got accepted for funding by the German Science Foundation (DFG).

With an overall funding of over 1 Mio Euro (with about 280.000 Euro for our group), we will develop together with our partner, the University Library Johann Christian Senckenberg, Frankfurt, an expert information service for the Judaica domain. Our group is mainly responsible for the data integration and enrichment.
