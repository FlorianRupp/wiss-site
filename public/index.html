LIS International Summer School 2016

Stuttgart Media University is pleased to announce the 2016 LIS Summer School which will be held at the Stuttgart Media University, Germany, on 19 - 24 September 2016.

We invite students of library and information science (LIS) and related fields to participate. The Summer School comprises six seminars focusing the topic “Designing the Future of Libraries, Learning and Information” and will be held at the Department of Library and Information Management.

WISS participates in the summer school and offers lectures on Semantic Web and Linked Data by Prof. Eckert, as well as Music Information Retrieval by our prospective visiting researcher Prof. Mari Itoh, Aichi Shukutoku University, Nagoya, Japan.

PhD defense of Thomas Hartmann

Mon, May 9, 2016

On July 8th, 2016, Thomas Hartmann will defend his PhD thesis at KIT, Karlsruhe. His thesis has the title “Validation Framework for RDF-based Constraint Languages” and is supervised by Kai Eckert and York Sure-Vetter from KIT.
